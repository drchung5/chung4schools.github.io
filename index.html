About David

A lifelong Iowan and 35-year Cedar Rapids resident, David and his wife raised eight children in our public schools. For 22 years, they were active Jefferson Show Choir parents, and David volunteered throughout the district—from sewing costumes to running concessions and coaching MathCounts.

With over three decades in the computer industry and experience teaching a semester-long programming class at a local high school, he understands both technology and education.

David is running for the Cedar Rapids School Board to bring independent, thoughtful leadership that listens to parents, supports teachers, and puts students first.

Election Information

David is running for School Board in District 2. All voters in the Cedar Rapids Community School District can vote for David, regardless of where they live.

Election Day is November 4, 2025.
